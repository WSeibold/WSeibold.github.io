Introductions

Originally from the metropolis of Waterloo, Iowa - the sixth largest city in Iowa and the most advanced city in the world that contains the zip code 50701. This is where the magic all began! Weston learned an impecable work ethic through his summers detasseling corn, built fortitude in winter igloo construction and listened to health dose of country music on the old dirt roads.

Academics

Weston graduated from the the highest ranked and most well known University ever to be based in a former but no longer current capital of Iowa, the legendary University of Iowa where he studied Finance, Economics, and Philosophy and expanded his knowledge of excellent life living outside the classroom. A lifelong Iowa Hawkeye fan, Weston endeavors to make an annual pilgrimage back to the Historic Kinnick Stadium.

Experience

Weston began his career in Chicago as an investment banking analyst and more recently as a private equity associate. Throughout his five year carreer prior to Kellogg, Weston helped advise on the acquisition of numerous companies and developed a diverse business acumen. A student of problem solving and intellectual challenge, post-MBA Weston will join the Boston Consulting Group as a full time consultant.
